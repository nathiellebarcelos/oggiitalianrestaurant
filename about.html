The Restaurant

Oggi was born from the idea of two open-minded friends who love to taste and experience the newest innovations in modern gastronomy, especially when it comes to the magnificent Italian cuisine.

From mixing the finest Italian traditions with unexpected ingredients to playing with different textures and seasonal flavours, we want to captivate you with our unique food creations delivered in the warmest way you could ever imagine.

Our newly innovated space was designed to make you feel as if you were having an excellent dinner in one of the charming restaurants of the Quadrilatero d’Oro.

Come join us for this remarkable experience!
